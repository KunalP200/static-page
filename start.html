Angela's Recipe for the Best

Cinnamon Rolls


Ingrediants

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flavour

    • For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

    • Instructions

    1. mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
    2. Melt the butter and add the mixture
    3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
    4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
    5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl
